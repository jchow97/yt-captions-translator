# YouTube Caption Translator Chrome Extension
## Overview
The "YouTube Caption Translator" is a feature-rich Chrome extension that allows users to translate YouTube video captions to their preferred language. By integrating seamlessly into the YouTube interface, this extension provides a smooth and intuitive experience for users who want to enjoy global content without language barriers.

## Key Features
- Real-Time Translation: Experience on-the-fly translation of video captions while watching YouTube videos.
- Language Selection: Choose from a wide range of supported languages for translation.
- *Translator Selection: Choose from various supported translators.
- Privacy and Security: The extension operates locally on your browser. It does not collect any personal or sensitive data.

## How to Use
Installation: To install the extension, follow these steps:

1. Download this repo to your local machine.
1. Open Google Chrome and go to "chrome://extensions/".
1. Enable Developer mode in the top-right corner of the page.
1. Click on "Load unpacked" and select the extension package folder.
1. Activation: Once the extension is installed, it will automatically integrate with your YouTube interface.

**Translation**: When watching a YouTube video with captions, the extension will display a language selection option (icon or dropdown) on the video player.

**Language** Selection: Click on the language dropdown and choose your preferred language for translation.

***Engine Selection**: Click on the engine dropdown and choose your preferred translator for translation.

**Privacy**: Rest assured that the extension operates solely on your browser and does not collect any personal information. Your data and privacy remain secure.
